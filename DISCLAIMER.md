# Disclaimer

This MCP server provides pest management data for informational purposes only.

## Not Professional Advice

The data served by this tool does not constitute professional pest management, agronomic, or pesticide application advice. Treatment recommendations, product approvals, and IPM thresholds are based on published Dutch government and research sources, but individual field conditions, resistance profiles, and local regulations vary. Always consult a qualified agronomist or licensed gewasbeschermingsadviseur before making crop protection decisions.

## Pesticide Data

Pesticide approval data is sourced from the Ctgb (College voor de toelating van gewasbeschermingsmiddelen en biociden) toelatingsbank. **Always check the current Ctgb register (https://toelatingen.ctgb.nl/) before purchasing or applying any pesticide product.** Product approvals can be revoked or amended at any time. This tool shows the data available at the time of its last ingestion run.

## Data Sources

All data is sourced from:

- **Ctgb toelatingsbank** -- College voor de toelating van gewasbeschermingsmiddelen en biociden
- **WUR/PPO Gewasbeschermingskennisbank** -- Wageningen University & Research
- **CLM Milieumeetlat** -- Centrum voor Landbouw en Milieu
- **NVWA** -- Nederlandse Voedsel- en Warenautoriteit
- **Aanbevelende Rassenlijst** -- Stichting CGO

All data is sourced from publicly available government and research publications.

## Currency

Pest management data changes. Product approvals are amended periodically. Resistance profiles shift over seasons. This tool shows the data available at the time of its last ingestion run. Check `check_data_freshness` for the age of the data.
